Welcome to SCPUC Library

Our library provides an ideal environment to cultivate reading habits among students. With a vast collection of over 7,000 books, it offers a wealth of knowledge to guide students toward a successful future. We place great emphasis on learning resources that help students excel in their academic and professional pursuits. Our library features specialized sections dedicated to competitive exams like KCET, NEET, JEE, and others. Additionally, we have incorporated a digital library, ensuring that users have access to the most current and relevant information at their fingertips.
